Guidelines and Rules for Striking

Here is a list of guidelines and rules to follow when striking in order for our message to be taken seriously:

  • 1. Do not go onto the road - stay within the designated area

  • 2. No violence

  • 3. No vandalism

  • 4. Do not bring drugs or alcohol

  • 5. Comply with police and follow their instructions

  • 6. Listen to stewards/event organisers and be respectful towards them and other strikers

  • 7. Be polite to the public - explain your aims and be calm and patient

  • 8. Do not annoy or distract drivers

  • 9. Know your rights
