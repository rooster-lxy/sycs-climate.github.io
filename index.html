Welcome to SYCS

Welcome to the page for Scottish Youth Climate Strikers. We are part of the FridaysForFuture movement started by Greta Thunberg.

Please use the navigation section at the top of the page to find information on our aims and guidelines, as well as strike information.

Information on next Strike FridaysForFuture

15 March

There is a "DEEP Protest" happening on 15 March in which cities from all over the world are taking part. You do not need to come to the entire protest so even staying for an hour or so is okay. Click here for more information.

How to deal with Schools

The City of Edinburgh Council has agreed to allow students to miss school to protest without punishing them. If you're coming to the Strike, just ask your parents to write a letter explaining that and hand it in to the school. If you're coming from outside of the City of Edinburgh Council area and/or go to a school not administered by the City of Edinburgh Council, this may not apply to you so enquire at school for clarification.
